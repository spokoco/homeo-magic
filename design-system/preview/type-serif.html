Nux vomica

Kent's Lectures on Homeopathic Materia Medica · 1897

The Nux patient is irritable, impatient, and cannot bear contradiction. Headache worse in the morning on waking, accompanied by a sense of fullness in the head. The mental state is one of strain — the overworked scholar, the sedentary businessman, the student of late nights and strong coffee.
